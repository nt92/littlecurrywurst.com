Spoon University

Spoon University is a leading college food & lifestyle publication with over 90 active chapters across the country. Mackenzie was editor of her Spoon chapter and worked for Spoon HQ.

Her20s

Hers20s is a subsection of Her Campus, the online publishing & digital marketing company based in Boston. Mackenzie writes about post-grad life and hacks to make your 20s easier.

Phoenix Crypto - Codecademy

Codecademy, the popular code learning platform, has a blog component that Mackenzie contributes articles about blockchain tech and cryptocurrencies to. Mackenzie is also the leader of the Phoenix Crypto Codecademy chapter.

She Shreds Magazine

She Shreds Magazine reports on female guitarists and bassists in the music world and "transcends boundaries like gender and genre." Mackenzie contributed to Issue #18.

Commentaries on the Gallic Wars

Mackenzie summarizes and analyzes each chapter from Julius Caesar's infamous, bloody narrative, "The Conquest of Gaul."

Rowdy Magazine

Rowdy is a cutting-edge publication from the University of Florida that features fashion, activism, cultural commentary and more. For Volume 3, Mackenzie contributed a lush recipe for a lavendar & gin cocktail.

Gainesville Sun

The Gainesville Sun is an independent newspaper located in Gainesville, Florida. Mackenzie contributed to the weekly column for "Self Narrate," a storytelling collective.

Tahoe Trail Bar

Tahoe Trail Bars were hatched on the shores of Lake Tahoe in 1955 and include flavors like caramel chocolate chunk, peanut butter chocolate, mango coconut, and dark chocolate cherry.

Narrow Magazine

Narrow is a local music & arts print magazine in Gainesville, FL. Mackenzie covered Heartwood Music Festival in Volume 2, Issue 1 and is currently crafting new content for future issues.

Reprise Magazine

Reprise Magazine was a "music, style, and culture magazine based in Gainesville, Florida" that was created by students at the University of Florida (currently inactive). It was under the umbrella of Swamp Records, a student run record label.

The Belly Button Chronicles: A Wild Mack & Thot Take on Life Together

This is Mackenzie's first self-published novel detailing the first year of her relationship with Head Coder In Charge, Nikhil Thota (thanks for the website babe!).

Vitae Romanorum: The Lives of the Romans

This is a collection of short stories featuring Ancient Roman characters, intrigues, and art. This was Mackenzie's first self-published work and spans centuries of ancient history.
