Hi, I'm Mackenzie! 🧼

I’m the CEO and co-founder of Hash Basis, a crypto native accounting firm based in San Francisco! I moved to SF a few years ago (all thanks to my fiancé, Nikhil), and I absolutely love living here. From hiking around Angel Island to rambling evening walks off Alamo Square, there’s never a shortage of good times and amazing friends.

Before starting Hash Basis, I led all revenue and crypto accounting at Figment, a crypto infrastructure provider. And before that, I was fresh out of college (Go Gators 🐊) working at Honeywell and doing revenue accounting for jet engines. It’s funny because I thought accounting was so droll in college - and now, I’m obsessed with it! I love Luca Pacioli (the father of accounting) and all of the messy problems I encounter daily at Hash Basis. I find great meaning in my work and even when I’m exhausted, there’s still a well of passion for my customers and profession.

When I’m not working, I’m doing a yoga teacher training program in SF and planning my wedding. I also love listening to audiobooks (I recently finished Three Body Problem) and hanging out with my nephews, the two sweetest and most rambunctious kiddos around.

Drop me a line at mackenzie@hashbasis.xyz or find me on LinkedIn. Have a lovely day!
